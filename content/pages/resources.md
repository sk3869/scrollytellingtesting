## Where to Learn More

The following organizations provide authoritative, up-to-date information on Alzheimer's disease, dementia research, and caregiving support.

## Clinical & Research Organizations

- **[Alzheimer's Association](https://www.alz.org)** — The leading voluntary health organization in Alzheimer's care, support, and research. Includes a 24/7 helpline and care resource finder.
- **[NIH National Institute on Aging](https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/alzheimers-and-dementia)** — U.S. government research hub with clinical trials, treatment guides, and caregiver resources.
- **[Alzheimer's Research UK](https://www.alzheimersresearchuk.org)** — The UK's leading dementia research charity, funding over 150 active projects.

## Landmark Studies Referenced

- **[Lancet Commission on Dementia Prevention 2020](https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lancet/article/PIIS0140-6736(20)30367-6/fulltext)** — The definitive review of modifiable risk factors, estimating up to 40% of cases are preventable.
- **[WHO Dementia Fact Sheet](https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/dementia)** — Global statistics and the WHO's public health response.
- **[Alzheimer's Association 2024 Facts & Figures](https://www.alz.org/alzheimers-dementia/facts-figures)** — Annual statistical report on prevalence, costs, and impact.

## Genetics

- **[APOE & Genetics — Alzheimer's Association](https://www.alz.org/alzheimers-dementia/what-is-alzheimers/causes-and-risk-factors/genetics)** — Plain-language overview of the APOE-ε4 gene and hereditary risk.
- **[NIA Alzheimer's Genetics Fact Sheet](https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/alzheimers-causes-and-risk-factors/alzheimers-disease-genetics-fact-sheet)** — Detailed breakdown of early-onset vs. late-onset genetic factors.

## Caregiving

- **[Alzheimer's Association Caregiver Center](https://www.alz.org/help-support/caregiving)** — Practical guides, support groups, and local resources for caregivers.
- **[Family Caregiver Alliance](https://www.caregiver.org/resource/alzheimers-disease/)** — Fact sheets and planning tools for families navigating a diagnosis.
